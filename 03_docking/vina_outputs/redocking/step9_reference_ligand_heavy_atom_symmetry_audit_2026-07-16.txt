Date: 2026-07-16
Software: RDKit 2025.09.5
Environment: docking_env

PURPOSE
Determine whether chemically equivalent heavy-atom permutations must be
considered when calculating redocking RMSD for the crystallographic
reference ligands QZN and OHN.

METHOD
Reference prepared SDF structures were read with RDKit.
Hydrogen atoms were removed.
Heavy-atom graph automorphisms were enumerated with chirality enabled.
Canonical atom symmetry classes were also inspected.

CONCLUSION
Neither QZN nor OHN contains non-trivial heavy-atom graph automorphisms
under the prepared molecular structures and retained stereochemical model.

Therefore, for both reference ligands:
- the explicit INDEX MAP correspondence is the only valid heavy-atom mapping;
- direct mapped heavy-atom RMSD equals symmetry-aware heavy-atom RMSD;
- no atom permutation can lower or otherwise alter the reported RMSD values.
